H. P. Langtangen


Oct 5, 2015


The documents below are generated from a common DocOnce source.

LaTeX

--latex_admon=mdfbox with blue sections and matching admon colors

--latex_admon=mdfbox with light blue background in mdfbox titles 

Terminal> doconce format pdflatex admon --latex_code_style=lst --latex_admon=mdfbox --latex_admon_color=bluestyle --latex_section_headings=blue

--latex_admon=mdfbox 

Terminal> doconce format pdflatex admon --latex_code_style=lst --latex_admon=mdfbox

--latex_admon=graybox2 

Terminal> doconce format pdflatex admon --latex_code_style=lst --latex_admon=graybox2

--latex_admon=grayicon 

Terminal> doconce format pdflatex admon --latex_code_style=lst --latex_admon=grayicon

--latex_admon=yellowicon 

Terminal> doconce format pdflatex admon --latex_code_style=lst --latex_admon=yellowicon

--latex_admon=colors1 

Terminal> doconce format pdflatex admon --latex_code_style=lst --latex_admon=colors1

--latex_admon=colors2 

Terminal> doconce format pdflatex admon --latex_code_style=lst --latex_admon=colors2

--latex_admon=paragraph-footnotesize 

Terminal> doconce format pdflatex admon --latex_code_style=lst --latex_admon=paragraph-footnotesize

--latex_admon=paragraph 

Terminal> doconce format pdflatex admon --latex_code_style=lst --latex_admon=paragraph
